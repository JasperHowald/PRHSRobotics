Home

Hello and welcome to the Palmer Ridge Robotics Team's home page.
 
Each year, our team, as well as others, receives a challenge that we must design a robot to complete. The game is different each time, and through it we learn about teamwork, engineering, programming, troubleshooting, and many other useful skills. Ryan, a team member who has been with us since the beginning, says, “It was interesting on how many kinds of devices were used to complete the objective in the game.”
